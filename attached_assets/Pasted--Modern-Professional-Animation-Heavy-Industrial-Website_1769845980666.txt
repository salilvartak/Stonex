

**(Modern, Professional, Animation-Heavy Industrial Website)**

---

### PROJECT OVERVIEW

Build a **modern, professional, animation-heavy corporate website** for an industrial minerals manufacturing company.

**Company Name:** Stonex Enterprises
**Industry:** Industrial Minerals – Fillers & Extenders
**Accent Color:** **#FFE600** (use only for highlights, CTAs, icons, hover states)
**Primary Colors:** Charcoal black, slate gray, off-white
**Tone:** Large-scale, trustworthy, export-ready manufacturer
**Style:** Clean, industrial, premium, confident

Animations should be **smooth, subtle, and purposeful**—no playful or gimmicky motion.
